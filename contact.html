Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and enrollment process.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Sunday to Thursday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. We are closed on Fridays.

How do I enroll my child in your programs?

Enrollment is a simple four-step process: inquiry, assessment, consultation, and final enrollment. You can start by contacting us through phone, email, or by filling out the contact form on our website.

Do you offer trial classes?

Yes, we offer trial classes to allow students to experience our teaching methods and environment before committing to a full program. Please contact us to schedule a trial class.

What curricula do you support?

We support various curricula including CBSE, IGCSE, IB, Federal Board, and Bangladesh Board. Our teachers are experienced in these educational systems and can provide tailored support.

How are classes structured?

Classes are typically structured with a mix of instruction, practice, and assessment. We focus on creating an interactive learning environment with personalized attention to each student's needs.
