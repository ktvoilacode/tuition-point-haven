About Us

Learn about our journey and commitment to educational excellence.

Our Story

Tuition Point Education Center was established with a clear vision: to provide high-quality, personalized education that helps students reach their full potential. Located in Al Wakra, Qatar, we serve students from kindergarten to grade 12 across various curricula.

Our center began with just a handful of dedicated teachers and a small group of students. Through our commitment to excellence and our personalized approach to education, we've grown into a trusted educational institution serving the community.

Today, Tuition Point is proud to be licensed by the Ministry of Education & Higher Education, offering comprehensive academic support across multiple curricula including CBSE, IGCSE, IB, Federal, and Bangladesh Board.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and confidence necessary to succeed academically and beyond. We strive to create a supportive learning environment where each student can discover their potential and develop a lifelong love for learning.

We are committed to providing personalized education that addresses the unique needs of each student, helping them overcome challenges and achieve their goals.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational center that transforms lives through education. We aim to be recognized for our innovative teaching methods, dedicated educators, and the academic success of our students.

We envision a future where every student who passes through our doors is equipped with the academic foundation and critical thinking skills needed to thrive in an increasingly complex world.
